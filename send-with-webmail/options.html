Have a suggestion for another provider?

You can leave this field as is unless you want to add a custom provider. mailto: links will be replace with this link, and any placeholders will be replaced with their equivilents from the original mailto: link (see below for replacement tokens).

You can use several placeholders in your webmail link:

  • %T - the to field
  • %S - the subject field
  • %M - the body field
  • %C - the CC field
  • %B - the BCC field

The uppercase placeholders are URI escaped and their lowercase versions are not (e.g. %T resolves to email%40domain.com and %t resolves to email@domain.com).
